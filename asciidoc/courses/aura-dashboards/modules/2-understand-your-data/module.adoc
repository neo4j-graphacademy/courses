== Module Overview

This module focuses on designing effective dashboards driven by your data model and business requirements.

You will learn how to create meaningful dashboards, tour the dashboards design canvas to understand how to represent your data visually.

You will learn how to:

* Describe your data model.
* Analyze your project structure.
* Design dashboards that align with stakeholder goals.
* Use GenAI to assist in dashboard design.
