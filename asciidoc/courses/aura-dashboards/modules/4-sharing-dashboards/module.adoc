= Collaboration and sharing

== Module Overview

It's time to share your movie recommendation dashboards with stakeholders! 
Dashboards become most valuable when content teams, marketing, and executives can access and interact with them to make data-driven decisions about recommendations and content acquisition.

This module covers the different ways to share dashboards, from inviting specific users to enabling project-wide access. You will also learn about user roles and permissions to ensure the right people have appropriate access levels.

You will learn how to:

* Share your movie recommendation dashboards with stakeholders and team members
* Use different sharing options available in Neo4j Dashboards 
* Apply best practices for collaboration and distribution of insights across your organization
