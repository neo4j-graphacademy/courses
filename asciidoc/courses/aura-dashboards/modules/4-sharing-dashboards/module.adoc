== Module Overview

It's time to share your insights with others! 
Dashboards become most valuable when stakeholders across your organization can access and interact with them.

This module covers the different ways to share dashboards, from inviting specific users to enabling project-wide access. You will also learn about user roles and permissions to ensure the right people have appropriate access levels.

You will learn how to:

* Share your dashboards with stakeholders and team members
* Use different sharing options available in Neo4j Dashboards 
* Apply best practices for collaboration and distribution of insights
