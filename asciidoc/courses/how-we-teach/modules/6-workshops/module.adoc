= Workshops
:order: 6

GraphAcademy delivers two distinct types of learning experience: self-paced online courses and instructor-led workshops. This module explains what workshops are, why they exist alongside courses, and how to manage them when they are scheduled.

In this module, you will learn:

* How workshops differ from online courses and why both exist
* What the practitioner workshop program is and how it is structured
* How to prepare sandboxes, create tracking issues, and notify the right people when a new training is scheduled
