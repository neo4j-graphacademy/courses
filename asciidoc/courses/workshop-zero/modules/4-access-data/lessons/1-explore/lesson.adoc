[.transcript-only]
=== Connect

Open the **Explore** tool and connect to your Aura instance:

console::Open Explore[tool=explore]

image::images/connect-annotated.png["Connect to instance"]

[.transcript-only]
=== Customers and Orders

You can use the search bar to create a _scene:_

image::images/search-scene-annotated.png["Search box highlighted"]

To find Orders and their related Customers:

. <click> in the searchbar
. <select> `Customer`
. <select> `PURCHASED`
. <select> `Order`

image::images/search-details.png["Search for Orders and Customers"]

Explore the results. Click on nodes and relationships to view their properties. Use the toolbar to change the layout, colors, and other visualization options.

[.transcript-only]
=== Experiment

Experiment with the search to find different parts of the graph, for example:

. Specific customers, such as [copy]#Alfreds Futterkiste#.
. Adding `Product` to the search to find out what products customers are purchasing.
. Finding which employee `SOLD` the orders to customers.
