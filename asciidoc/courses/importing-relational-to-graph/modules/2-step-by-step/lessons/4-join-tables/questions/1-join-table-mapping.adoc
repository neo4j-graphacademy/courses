[.question]
= Join Table to Relationship

When mapping a relational join table (e.g. `order_details` with columns `order_id`, `product_id`, `quantity`) to a graph, what happens to the `quantity` column?

Select the correct answer.

* [ ] It becomes a property on the Order node
* [ ] It becomes a property on the Product node
* [x] It becomes a property on the relationship between Order and Product
* [ ] It is dropped because only foreign keys are mapped

[TIP,role=hint]
.Hint
====
Join tables become relationship types; columns that are not foreign keys describe the link and become relationship properties.
====

[TIP,role=solution]
.Solution
====
**It becomes a property on the relationship between Order and Product.**

The join table becomes a relationship type (e.g. `CONTAINS`). The foreign key columns (`order_id`, `product_id`) identify the nodes to connect; any other column, such as `quantity`, describes the relationship and becomes a relationship property.
====
